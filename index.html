Hi, I'm Jiyoung You.

Juvenile Justice Adolescent Risk Behavior Social Inequality Prevention Science Youth Reintegration

I am a psychology researcher whose work sits at the intersection of developmental science, juvenile justice, and social policy. My research examines how social inequality and developmental processes shape adolescent risk behavior and justice involvement. I am particularly interested in understanding pathways to resilience and positive outcomes among justice-involved youth through evidence-based prevention and intervention strategies.
